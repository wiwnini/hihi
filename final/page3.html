What Is Menhera
Menhera (メンヘラ)* , a Japanese slang term from the word “Mental Healther”.

This word is often associated with yami-kawaii (病みかわいい”)
; also translated to sick cute.

The kanji character ‘ 病’ is interpreted with the meaning of illness or sickness,and also the word mental illness, hence the associanciance of the word menhera. The word ‘menhera’ is often taken as an aesthetic, art style or either a fashion sense. There is also a character named Menhera-chan who is an unofficial mascot for this culture as her characteristics and whole character is made out from this subculture.
